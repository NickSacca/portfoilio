Scratch Project

This game was made using scratch from Me and my partner Nithin. This was a group effort project that went from the date 8/29 to 9/7. You can find the oringal project here you can also find our project notebook here. In this game your playing as a ball and you have to dodge obstacles to get to the finish flag. There are a total of 3 levels you need to complete in order to finish the game. Touching the moving object on the second level will trigger a backwards motion with another obstacle appearing. Touching any sort of gray will cause the game to be over and you have to restart.
